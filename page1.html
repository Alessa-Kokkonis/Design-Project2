How Early Should You Start Studying?

Studies have shown that the best time to start studying is two to three weeks before an exam and five to ten days before a test.The worst thing to do is only study the night before since your brain has not had enough time to procces the information. It is also sugested that you review the material you learned in class every night in order to retain it. It is best to study for about 1-2 hours at a time so that you can remember the material better..
