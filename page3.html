How to balance studying and personal life?

The key to this is not to procrastinate and leave things pile up.Time management plays an essential role in accademic success while leaving time for other things.It is important to prioritize your tasks from most to least important that way if something is not completed it i not the end of the world.Lastly set goals for yourself and reward yourself when you have obtained them.
